I have always had the attitude that tech shouldn’t be alienating, but should be inclusive and empower individuals to take whatever they are passionate about to the next level. I’ve witness many a Luddite rebuke powerful tools at their disposal due to feeling overwhelmed and intimidated. Users should never feel like their tech is working against them or questioning their intelligence. It’s the duty of good UX to break down barriers and make tech make sense.

I was immediately intrigued when I first learned of the field of UX. It combines my empathy for making tech more accessible, my passion for researching subject matter soup to nuts, and my desire to inject more creativity from my musically inclined personal life into my work life. I believe UX can better the world one strategically placed button at a time.
